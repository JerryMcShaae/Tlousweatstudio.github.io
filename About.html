About Sweat Studio

At Tlou Sweat Studio, we believe in empowering individuals through fitness. Our world-class equipment, certified trainers, and vibrant community help you reach your goals.

My name is Tlou, and I started Sweat Studio because I kept hearing the same thing from people—how do I lose belly fat? But it wasn’t just about the fat. I realized, through my own journey, that so many of us are fighting battles no one talks about—whether it’s addiction, negative thoughts, or health struggles. I’ve faced my share: overeating, alcohol, weed, cigarettes, and health issues. And while I still face some of these challenges, working out has been my way to fight back. When I started, it wasn’t just about changing my body—it was about changing my mindset. It was about building the discipline to fight against my own battles and find a sense of purpose, even on the toughest days. Sweat Studio was born out of a desperation for change. It’s not just about fat loss—it’s about real change. It’s a place where you can work on more than your body. You can work on your mind, your habits, and your life. I created this space for people like me—people who want to feel better, live better, look better, and take back control, one rep at a time.

Sweat Studio: Where sweat shapes greatness and dreams come true.

TLOU means Elephant a symbol of strength, wisdom, endurance, and sacred presence. Just like the elephant, our approach is steady, powerful, and rooted in deep spiritual alignment. We don't rush results - we build foundations that last.
